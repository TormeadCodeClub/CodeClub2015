ROBIN HOOD AND THE SHERWOOD HOODIES!

This is performed by Yr 6 at Tormead Junior School!

We have put together a production of Robin Hood and the Sherwood Hodies! Everyone in the year, has contributed, including the parents who have had amazing patience for the costume shopping and line learning. Although Mrs Fry is the real star of our show. Being calm and controlled during the scenes we had to repeat about 50 times. Mrs Fry has shown us what we are capable of if we really try, and every single one of us owe our great performance to her.

Why Should You Watch Our Play?

You should definetely watch our version of Robin Hood and The Sherwood Hoodies as we shall make you laugh, cry, be angry, nervous, excited and so many other emotions you shall be emotianally exhausted. There are jokes on every page, and everyone participates with energy and enthusiasm.
