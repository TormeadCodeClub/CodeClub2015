KEY STAGE ONE SPORTS DAY

Introduction and The Events

Introduction

Sports day is children's favourite event in the year as it is a competition between the four houses. Key stage one's sports day activities are different to the others so we'll give you a brief look at what they will be doing!

The Events

The 5 events in key stage 1's sports day are:

  1. The Skipping Race! In the skipping race, four girls, representing their houses, skip confidentaly to the finish!
  2. The Running race! Similar to the skipping race, but four girls run approximately 10 metres!
  3. Racket and Ball race! This is like an egg and spoon race but with a racket and ball!
  4. Rugby ball race! They ran with a rugby ball!
  5. Novelty race! Using sacks, Key stage one JUMPED across the Urn Field!
